Hemlock

Conium maculatum

Definition: A poisonous plant associated in floriography with death.

Meaning
Death
Origin
Hemlock is a poisonous plant that causes paralysis and death. Perhaps the most infamous poisoning by hemlock was that of Socrates, who drank a tea made from the plant after being sentenced to death for his moral philosophy.
Pair With
  • Chrysanthemum for condolences upon the loss of a loved one
  • Nettle for a loved one who has been taken away too soon
