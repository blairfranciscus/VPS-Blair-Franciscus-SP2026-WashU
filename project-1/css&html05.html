Ibby's Bistro

Danforth University Center · WashU
Lunch: Mon–Fri 11–3 · Staff: Brenda & Josh
Ibby’s Bistro celebrates fresh, creative cuisine inspired by local ingredients and seasonal flavors. Named for Elizabeth “Ibby” Danforth, former first lady of WashU, the bistro honors her legacy of care for students and community. Ibby also had a butterfly garden, and this diner is an homage to her love for butterflies.
