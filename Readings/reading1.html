Restaurant Websites

January 14, 2026

Reading this interview about restaurant websites was incredibly interesting to think about, as so much has changed over the last seven years. From 2019 to 2026, menus have had to go from mostly on paper to almost all online with QR code menus.

Additionally, food social media websites have gotten pretty popular, like the app Beli. I enjoyed the thought-provoking process of asking why a restaurant would need a website in the first place. Is this a place to show the website's personality, or is it simply a means to an end for a Wednesday night food exploration for a couple in St. Louis?

I think of myself as a mostly practical person and have only visited restaurant websites in the past to order food, peek at the menu, or find a reservation. I had never thought about wanting to share a restaurant's ambiance in the website before.

I love the idea of letting patrons peek inside the restaurant digitally by giving them a preview on the website of what to expect when they come to enjoy their meal there. Overall, I enjoyed the process of analyzing why restaurants have websites, since it is something I had never given much thought before.
