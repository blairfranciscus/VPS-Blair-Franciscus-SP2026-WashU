# Ibby's Bistro

## About Ibby's
Located in the Danforth University Center,
Ibby’s Bistro celebrates fresh, creative
cuisine inspired by local ingredients and
seasonal flavors. Named for Elizabeth
“*Ibby*” Danforth, former first lady of
WashU, the bistro honors her legacy of care
for students and community. Each dish
reflects a commitment to sustainability,
local partnerships, and culinary excellence
—making Ibby’s more than a restaurant, but
a true part of the WashU experience.
