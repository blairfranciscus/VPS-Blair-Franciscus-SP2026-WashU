Bluebell

Hyacinthoides

Definition: A flower associated in floriography with humility and faithfulness.

Meaning
  • Humility
  • Faithfulness
Origin
The bluebell's appearance inspired its associations with humility and faithfulness. The tranquil, bell-shaped flowers bow down on the stem, shying away from the sunshine as though showing contrition.
Pair With
  • Peony for forgiveness for violating societal norms
  • Passionflower as a gift for someone preparing for a religious sacrament
