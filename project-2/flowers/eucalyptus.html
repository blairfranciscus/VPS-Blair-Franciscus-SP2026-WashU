Eucalyptus

Eucalyptus

Definition: A plant associated in floriography with protection and safeguarding from harm.

Meaning
Protection
Origin
Aboriginal peoples used eucalyptus oil to disinfect, soothe, and treat many common ailments, thus providing protection from illness. Named by Charles Louis L'Heritier de Brutelle in 1788, eucalyptus's name comes from the Greek roots eu and kalyptos, translating to "well" and "covered," again suggesting protection.
Pair With
  • Heather for good luck to a friend embarking on a journey
  • Queen Anne's lace to wish the recipient safe travels
