Ibby's Bistro

Danforth University Center · WashU
Lunch: Mon–Fri 11–3 · Staff: Brenda & Josh
Ibby’s Bistro celebrates fresh, creative cuisine inspired by local ingredients and seasonal flavors. Named for Elizabeth “Ibby” Danforth, former first lady of WashU, the bistro honors her legacy of care for students and community. Ibby also had a butterfly garden, and this diner is an homage to her love for butterflies.

Ibby’s Corner

Did you know?

The Elizabeth Gray Danforth Butterfly Garden was dedicated in October 1996 in her memory.

Open 24/7

It’s designed as a quiet campus pause spot, with benches, an arbor, and butterfly-friendly plants.

WashU first lady

Ibby was WashU’s first lady from 1971–1995, known for warmth and care for students.

Legacy in action

The Danforth Scholars Program supports students known for leadership in service + character.
