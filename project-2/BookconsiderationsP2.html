Project Frame

P2. Book -> Website runs across six weeks (February to April). The goal is to translate a systematic, visual, and multi-voiced book into a website while preserving its ethos and curating content for legibility, navigation, and memorable experience across desktop and mobile.

This page is the process archive for your decisions, weekly iterations, and final production structure.
